How to Play

  • Arrow Keys: Move paddle left and right
  • Space: Pause/Resume game
  • Green towers (Left): Bid orders - highest to lowest price
  • Red towers (Right): Ask orders - lowest to highest price
  • Goal: Destroy all individual blocks from the top-aligned towers!
  • Each block represents 0.1 units of the cryptocurrency
  • Towers grow downward from the top of the orderbook area
